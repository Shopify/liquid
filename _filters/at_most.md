Limits a number to a maximum value.

<p class="code-label">Input</p>
```liquid
{%- raw -%}
{{ 4 | at_most: 5 }}
{{ 4 | at_most: 3 }}
{% endraw %}
```

<p class="code-label">Output</p>
```text
{{ 4 | at_most: 5 }}
{{ 4 | at_most: 3 }}
```
